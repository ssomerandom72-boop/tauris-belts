Our Story

An Ethos of Excellence Crafted Since 1978

Tauris Belts and Custom Leathers was established in 1978 by Philip P. Taurisano, a former Utica, New York police officer.

We create high-quality leather products that last a lifetime, carrying the hallmark of over four decades of American craftsmanship.
