Chapter I — Our Story

An Ethos of Excellence Crafted Since 1978

Tauris Belts and Custom Leathers was established in 1978 by Philip P. Taurisano, a former Utica, New York police officer. Philip worked alongside his brother, Michael P. Taurisano, who operated the Tauris Holsters brand since the mid-1960s.

While collaborating with Michael — who also served as the Deputy Chief of the Utica Police Department — Philip developed a firm conviction: every officer and legal weapon carrier needed a high-quality leather belt to properly contain any holster or apparatus carrying their weapon.

The Process

The Art of Meticulous Handling

A Heritage Since 1978

Our process is anchored in the belief that true luxury requires time. Every hide is inspected for depth and durability, then hand-cut and stitched using techniques that have remained unchanged since our founding in 1978. We don't just manufacture leather goods; we build companions that carry the legacy of over four decades of expertise.
